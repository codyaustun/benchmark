Deep Learning Benchmark

What is DAWNBench?

Most research from the deep learning community has concentrated on improving the accuracy of deep learning models on various tasks like image classification, question answering, and object recognition. However, a lot of questions remain on how long it takes to train these models, how much it costs, and how fast inference can be performed on the trained models.

DAWNBench is an effort from Stanford DAWN to answer some of these questions. We're still in the early stages of developing this benchmark, and looking for people to add their own models to the benchmark -- instructions on how to do this are here!
